Services

What
we do

Every service is built around one goal — making your Edinburgh business impossible to ignore online.

01 Web Design & Development

Custom-built websites that look like they cost ten times what they do. We handle everything — from visual design through to clean, fast code deployed on Cloudflare's global network. Mobile-perfect. Impossible to ignore.

02 Brand Identity & Strategy

A visual identity that resonates with your audience and stands apart from every competitor in Edinburgh. Logos, colour palettes, typography systems — that work everywhere from your website to your signage.

03 SEO & Performance

Every site we build is Edinburgh-SEO-ready from day one — structured data, sitemaps, Google Search Console setup, and Core Web Vitals optimisation. Built for search without ever compromising the experience.

04 E-Commerce & Bookings

Booking systems, online stores, Stripe payments. We connect your website to the tools your business already uses — Square, Squire, Fresha, Shopify — and make every customer interaction seamless.

05 Hosting & Support

Managed hosting on Cloudflare Pages — globally fast, always on. Monthly retainers include content updates, performance reviews, and priority support so your site stays as sharp as the day it launched.
